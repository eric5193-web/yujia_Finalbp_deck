Research Approach
"I'm a believer that you don't have to buy the most expensive things to look good. It's all about how you put it together."
Instagram  ·  lifestyle creator
The Key Insight
It's not luxury fatigue — it's sameness fatigue. Quiet luxury makes everyone blend in. The conversation rewards standing out as yourself.

Yujia's opening: you don't have to flatten yourself to belong.
How We Listened
Digital ethnography via Talkwalker, 326.6K mentions.

Quant dashboards, a 20,000-post extract, and a Reddit deep-dive. Searched for recurring themes, then pulled representative quotes.
