## About

@Yujia is a lifestyle creator documenting life as a Chinese woman fully at home in New York — active across Instagram, TikTok/Douyin, and Xiaohongshu. *East Meets Feed* is an organic campaign strategy built from ethnographic research, designed to grow her combined audience to 5,000 engagements without paid promotion.
